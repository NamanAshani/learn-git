Streamline Your Tile Business with TileFlow

TileFlow is a comprehensive tile management solution designed to help companies efficiently manage inventory, dispatch, employees, and operations from a single dashboard. Simplify your workflow and boost productivity with our intuitive platform.

Powerful Features for Your Business

TileFlow offers a complete suite of tools designed specifically for tile management companies to streamline operations and maximize efficiency.

Inventory Management

Track tile inventory in real-time, manage stock levels, set reorder points, and generate detailed inventory reports.

Dispatch & Logistics

Efficiently manage deliveries, assign drivers, track shipments, and optimize routes for timely tile deliveries.

Employee Management

Manage staff schedules, track attendance, assign tasks, and monitor performance across all departments.

Analytics & Reporting

Gain insights with comprehensive dashboards, sales reports, and performance analytics to drive business growth.

Order & Invoice Management

Process orders, generate invoices, track payments, and manage customer accounts seamlessly.

Operations Dashboard

Centralized control panel to monitor all aspects of your tile business from a single interface.
